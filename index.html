About us

Baby Corp is a large corporation located in a heavenly realm.

Baby Corp is responsible for the creation of all babies and their primary goal is to make sure that babies are the most loved creatures on earth.

Baby Corp possesses a giant, self-editing pie chart that shows how all the love in the world is divided up. The sections of the pie chart grow and shrink in real time in accordance with the distribution of love on earth.

All the babies who come from Baby Corp initially remember it, but lose those memories when their pacifier is taken away.
